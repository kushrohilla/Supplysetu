# Retailer Onboarding Workflow: Step-by-Step Guide

## Overview

This guide ensures retailers **place their first order during the initial onboarding visit** (target: 45–75 minutes total). It combines salesman guidance, technical assistance, and UX optimization to move retailers from discovery → account setup → first order → success celebration.

**Primary Goal:** First order placement = instant value + adoption momentum  
**Target Completion Time:** 45–75 minutes (first visit)  
**Success Rate Target:** ≥85% of retailers place first order same-day

---

## PHASE 1: PRE-ARRIVAL PREPARATION (24–48h before visit)

### 1.1 Confirmation Call Script (Sales Rep or Onboarding Team)

**Timing:** 24h before scheduled visit  
**Duration:** 5–10 minutes  
**Purpose:** Confirm commitment, gather context, set expectations

---

**[Sales Rep Opening]**

> "Hi [Retailer Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. I'm excited about our onboarding session tomorrow at [TIME]. I'm calling to confirm a few things so we make the absolute best use of our time together.

> **Quick questions:**
> 1. Will [Primary Contact] be available tomorrow, or should I know about other team members joining?
> 2. Do you have a smartphone or tablet we can use to download and test the app? (Android or iPhone?)
> 3. What products do you typically order most? (E.g., dairy, frozen, beverages?) I want to make sure we show you relevant items.
> 4. Any concerns or questions I should know about before we meet?

> **Here's what we'll do tomorrow:**
> - Get you set up on the app (takes about 10 minutes)
> - Walk through the platform together (15 minutes)
> - Place your first order live (20–30 minutes)
> - You'll have your order confirmation in hand before I leave

> Sound good? Great. See you tomorrow at [TIME] at [LOCATION]."

## PHASE 2: ARRIVAL & INTRODUCTION (5–10 minutes)

### 2.1 Salesman Introduction Script

**Setting:** Retailer's office/store  
**Duration:** 5–10 minutes  
**Attendees:** You (Onboarding Rep) + Primary Contact ± 1–2 team members  
**Goal:** Build rapport, explain session flow, manage expectations

---

**[Warm Greeting & Rapport Building]**

> "Hi [Name], great to see you! Thanks for taking the time today. I'm [Your Name], and I'll be walking you through our platform. Before we dive in, I wanted to cover what we're doing today so you know exactly what to expect.

> **Quick intro:** I've been onboarding retailers like you for [#] months, and I've seen firsthand how much time this platform saves on ordering. I'm here to make sure you have a smooth, successful experience."

---

**[Explain the Session Agenda]**

> "Here's what we'll cover in the next hour:

> 1. **App Setup (10 min):** We'll download the app on your phone and get you logged in. Takes just a minute.
> 2. **Quick Platform Tour (15 min):** I'll show you the dashboard, catalog, and how to search for products.
> 3. **Your First Order (30 min):** Here's the important part—we'll place your first real order together. You'll see the order confirmation before I leave, and it'll be on its way to you.
> 4. **Q&A & Support (5 min):** Any questions at all? I'll leave you with my contact info and support resources.

> **My goal today:** You walk away with a placed order and confidence that you can use this on your own. Sound good?"

---

**[Manage Expectations & Address Device Access]**

> "One quick thing—I brought my tablet as a backup, but it's much better if we use your own device so you're familiar with it. Do you have a smartphone handy we can test with?"

> **[If 'yes']** "Perfect. iPhone or Android?"  
> **[If 'no']** "No problem. We can use my tablet today, and then I'll send you a QR code link to download on your phone after we're done. Same end result."

---

### 2.2 Device Setup Options

**Option A: Retailer's Personal Device (Preferred)**
- [ ] Ask: "Is your phone (Android/iPhone) with you?"
- [ ] Ask: "Can I install the app on it quickly?"
- [ ] Advantage: Retailer learns on *their* device; immediate next-use confidence

**Option B: Onboarding Rep's Tablet (Backup)**
- [ ] Use rep's tablet for walkthrough today
- [ ] Send retailer QR + setup instructions post-visit
- [ ] Advantage: No delays from app download/login on slower connection
- [ ] Disadvantage: Lower tactile learning; higher re-onboarding risk

**Option C: Hybrid (Best for Low-Tech Retailers)**
- [ ] Use rep's tablet for tour + first order placement
- [ ] Rep does login on retailer's phone *while* explaining
- [ ] By end, both devices are set up; retailer can repeat same process tomorrow

**→ Recommendation:** Push for Option A. If retailer hesitates, explain: "Takes 60 seconds to download, and I'll send you a link. Easiest if I'm here to help."

## PHASE 3: APP INSTALLATION & LOGIN (10 minutes)

### 3.1 App Download & Installation Steps

**Scenario A: Using Retailer's Smartphone (Preferred)**

#### For iPhone (iOS)

1. **Open the App Store**
   - From home screen, tap the blue "App Store" icon (looks like staircase)
   - Tap "Search" tab at the bottom
   
2. **Search for the App**
   - Tap search bar at top
   - Type: "[Platform Name]" (e.g., "SupplySetu")
   - Tap the app from results (verify logo/publisher)
   
3. **Download & Install**
   - Tap "Get" button (cloud icon)
   - May ask for Face ID / Touch ID / Apple password
   - Tap "Install" → Wait 30–45 seconds for download
   - Tap "Open" (or home screen → tap app icon)
   
4. **Check Installation**
   - App should open to login screen
   - Icon now appears on home screen

**Rep Guidance Script (iOS):**

> "Okay, tap the App Store icon—that blue one with the stairs. Got it? Now tap 'Search' at the bottom. Type in '[Platform Name].' Yep, that one. Now hit 'Get' and it'll ask for your Face ID. [Wait.] Great, it's downloading. You should see 'Open' in just a second... There we go! Perfect."

---

#### For Android

1. **Open Google Play Store**
   - From home screen, tap the colorful "Google Play Store" icon (looks like triangle)
   - If not visible, swipe up → scroll to find "Google Play Store"
   
2. **Search for the App**
   - Tap search bar at top ("Search apps & games")
   - Type: "[Platform Name]"
   - Tap the app from results (verify logo/publisher)

3. **Download & Install**
   - Tap "Install" button
   - May ask for permissions (location, contacts) → tap "No" or "Allow" as instructed
   - Wait 20–30 seconds for download
   - Tap "Open" when ready

4. **Check Installation**
   - App opens to login screen
   - Icon appears on home screen or in app drawer

**Rep Guidance Script (Android):**

> "On Android, tap the Google Play Store icon—the colorful triangle. Search for '[Platform Name].' There it is. Tap 'Install' and—don't worry about all these permission boxes—just tap the 'X' or 'No' for now; we'll configure later if needed. [Wait 30 sec.] Great! Now tap 'Open' and you're in."

---

**Scenario B: Using QR Code (If App Pre-Download Needed)**

**Prepare a QR code** linking to app store (iOS/Android-specific):

```
iOS App Store Link: https://apps.apple.com/app/[platform-id]
Android Google Play Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=[package-id]
```

Generate QR code → Print & include in onboarding packet.

**Script:**

> "We can also just scan this QR code [show code], and it'll take you straight to the download. You can do that later on WiFi at home if you prefer."

### 3.2 First Login Walkthrough

**Time:** 2–3 minutes  
**Goal:** Retailer authenticated, sees dashboard

---

**[Credential Handoff]**

> "Now, login credentials. I've pre-created your account, so everything's ready. Here's your username and a temporary password."

**[Write down:]**
- Username: [e.g., retailer@email.com or RETAILER_ID]
- Temporary Password: [e.g., Temp#2024!XYZ]
- Support Email: [support@company.com]
- Support Phone: [+1-XXX-XXX-XXXX]

> "Tap the username field, paste or type this in. Now password field → paste the temporary one. Tap 'Login' or 'Sign In.'"

---

**[Upon Successful Login]**

> "Perfect! You're in. You should see your dashboard now with your name and order history (empty for now, which is expected). Notice the tabs at the bottom? 'Home,' 'Catalog,' 'Orders,' 'Account.' We're about to use all of these."

---

**[If Login Fails]**

**Common Issues & Quick Fixes:**

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "Username/password incorrect" | Check for CAPS LOCK. Retry slower. If still fails, restart app. |
| "Connection error" | Check WiFi/cellular. Load retailer's browser → test [website] to confirm internet. |
| "Account not found" | Call Tech Support hotline. Usually resolved in 1–2 min via system check. |
| "App crashes on login" | Force quit app (swipe up on iOS, long-press on Android) → restart → try again. |

**[If Technical Issue Persists]**

> "No problem. This happens occasionally. I'm going to call our tech team—they can fix this in about 5 minutes while we grab coffee. [Call tech support.] ... Okay, they're resetting your account on their end. [Wait.] Try logging in again. [Attempt.] There we go! You're all set."

### 4.3 First Order Item Selection

**Duration:** 3–5 minutes  
**Goal:** Retailer has 3–5 items in cart, ready to order

---

**[Transition to Order Placement]**

> "Okay, now let's build your first order. I want you to place an order today with real items you'd actually order. That way, when you get the delivery, you'll know the system works and feel confident ordering again tomorrow.

> **Here's what I want you to do:** Which products do you typically order in your first weekly shipment? Think about your top 3–5 items. Could be milk, bread, beverages, frozen items—whatever. Let's find them and add them to your cart."

---

**[Guide Item Selection]**

**Rep Role:**
- Retailer states product need (e.g., "We need 2-liter milk")
- You guide search: "Type '2-liter' in the search. See the options?"
- Retailer selects one
- You explain quantity/pricing: "Each unit is [#] units per case. Price is [$/unit]. Want to start with 2 cases?"
- Retailer taps "Add to Cart"
- **Repeat 2–4 times** (target: 3–5 items for credible first order)

**Sample Dialog:**

> **You:** "Okay, what's item #1 you'd like to order?"
> 
> **Retailer:** "Milk. 2-liter."
> 
> **You:** "Perfect. Search for '2-liter milk.' [Retailer searches.] See these options? Usually we recommend the [Brand X] one—best price for volume. Tap on it. [Retailer taps.] Okay, details: each case has 6 units, costs $18/case. Want to start with 2 cases?"
> 
> **Retailer:** "Yeah, 2 cases."
> 
> **You:** "Great. See the qty field? Change it to 2 and tap 'Add to Cart.' Notice the green checkmark? That means it's now in your cart. [Retailer adds.] Perfect. Next item?"

---

**▶ Pro Tips for Item Selection:**

1. **Let retailer choose:** Builds ownership. Avoid "Let me pick for you."
2. **Suggest 1-2 bestsellers:** "Most retailers in your area order [Product X]—it's popular, good value."
3. **Mix product types:** Dairy + frozen + beverage (3+ distinct items) → looks like real order, boosts confidence.
4. **Keep quantities realistic:** Don't oversell. "2 cases" feels more credible than "10."
5. **Show diversity in cart:** If possible, different suppliers/brands → shows catalog breadth.

## PHASE 5: FIRST ORDER PLACEMENT (20–30 minutes)

### 5.1 Cart Review & Order Confirmation

**Duration:** 5–10 minutes  
**Goal:** Retailer reviews cart, confirms items, sees order summary

---

**[Move to Order Summary]**

> "Alright, you've got [#] items in your cart. Let's review them before we submit. Tap the 'Orders' tab and then 'Cart' or 'Review Order.'"

**[On Cart/Review Screen]**

**Point out:**

| Element | What to Say |
|---------|------------|
| **Cart Items** | "These are your [3–5] items. See quantities, unit price, total per item." |
| **Subtotal** | "Add up your items → Subtotal. This is before taxes and delivery." |
| **Delivery Options** | "Choose delivery method: [Standard (FREE, 5–7 days) / Express (2–3 days, +$5) / Priority (1 day, +$15)]. First-time customers often try Standard—safe, keeps costs down." |
| **Delivery Address** | "Is your address correct here? [Retailer confirms.] Good. If you have multiple locations, you'd select which one gets this delivery." |
| **Taxes & Fees** | "System auto-calculates based on your address. No surprise fees—it's all shown here before you confirm." |
| **Promo Code** (if applicable) | "Got a welcome code? Enter it here. [Enter code if provided.] Watch—discount applied instantly. Pretty cool." |
| **Order Total** | "Your final total is [$]. This is what you'll be charged and what your first shipment costs." |

---

**[Addressing Cart Objections]**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "That seems expensive for delivery." | "Standard delivery is free—that $X is just for [taxes/express option]. Standard takes 5–7 days; most retailers are fine with that." |
| "I want to add more items." | "No problem. Tap the search bar, add more. Or let's place your first order today, and you can add more next week. Either way." |
| "I'm not sure about quantities." | "Good instinct. Start conservative. You can reorder next week if you run out. Better to err on the safe side first order." |
| "Can I change the delivery address?" | "Absolutely. Tap on the address field, and you can change it to any of your registered locations—or add a new one." |

---

**Duration:** 5–7 minutes  
**Goal:** Payment captured, order submitted successfully

---

---

**[Payment Entry]**

**Scenario A: Card on File (Pre-Set by Admin)**

**Scenario B: New Card Entry**

> - Card number
> - Expiry date
> - CVV (3 digits on back)
> - Billing address (often auto-fills from account)
>
> [Retailer enters.] Tap 'Save' and then 'Complete Order.'"

---

**[Final Confirmation Screen]**

> "Perfect! You should see a confirmation screen with your order number. [Read number aloud.] Screenshot this or write it down. This is your reference if you ever need to track or modify."

**[Show Key Confirmation Details]:**
- Order #: [XXXXX]
- Order Date/Time: [timestamp]
- Expected Delivery: [date range]
- Total Amount Charged: [$]
- Items Summary: [list]
- Tracking Link: "You'll get an email with a tracking link. You can also check status anytime in the 'Orders' tab → 'Active Orders.'"

---

**[Common Checkout Issues & Fixes]**

| Issue | Resolution |
|-------|-----------|
| Payment declined | "Let me try again or use a different card on file. [Retry.] If it keeps declining, call your bank—sometimes they block online orders as fraud protection." |
| Email not confirming | "Check your spam folder first. If still not there, give me your phone # and I'll have support send an SMS confirmation." |

## PHASE 6: OBJECTION HANDLING & REASSURANCE (Ad-hoc, during session)

### 6.1 Common Retailer Objections & Responses

**Note:** See Section 6 of the main companion document [Common Retailer Objection Handling Notes](./retailer-onboarding-objection-handling.md) for detailed scripts. *Quick reference below.*

| Objection | Category | 1-Line Response | Dive-Deeper Script |
|-----------|----------|---|---|
| "I've got a system that works fine." | Complacency | "Absolutely. This adds to what you have—not replaces." | "I get it. Most retailers tell me the same thing initially. But after they use this for two weeks, they tell me they're saving 15–20 min per order. That's 1+ hour a week. Imagine that time back." |
| "What if I have problems?" | Trust | "You've got direct support—me, email, phone." | "Great Q. Most issues are solved in seconds (wrong product qty, address change). If there's a real problem, you call [phone] and we pick up. Rare, but we're here." |
| "Is there a contract?" | Risk | "Nope. Cancel anytime, no penalty." | "Smart to ask. No lock-in. You try it, if it's not for you, stop using it. But I'm confident you'll love it." |
| "What about my payment info security?" | Safety | "Industry-standard encryption. Your data is safer here than with email." | "We're PCI-compliant (that's the credit card security standard). Your data is encrypted end-to-end. You're more at risk emailing payment info to us than using this platform." |
| "I prefer ordering by phone." | Habit | "You can still do that if you want. This is another option." | "Totally fair. Keep phone ordering if you love it. But I think you'll find this faster—especially for browsing or if you want to reorder your usual items." |
| "Is this going to cost me money?" | Price | "First order's on the platform free. No app fees ever." | "No hidden fees. Prices are the same as phone/email. You're just paying for products + standard delivery. Nothing extra." |
